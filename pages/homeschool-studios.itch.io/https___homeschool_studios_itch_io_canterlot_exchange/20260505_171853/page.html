Canterlot Exchange

A downloadable game




You may be asking yourself: “Why attend Canterlot High?”


Well, for starters...

Because Canterlot High is no ordinary school! No, no, no! This is an environment where you will be presented with the opportunity to make cherished memories, form lifelong friendships, and experience exciting adventures you wouldn’t find anywhere else!

That being said, you may have to adapt to… certain… unique… situations cropping up from time to time….

But don't think of them as “weird” or “frightening” or as an “inconvenience”, but rather as a… learning experience!

Anyhow, now with that out of the way, let’s meet some of the wonderful students and faculty that make Canterlot High great!





As you get settled in with your host family, you may feel a little overwhelmed at first, but we’re confident you’ll get the hang of it all in no time!

With an open mind, a little patience, and the right friends, you’ll experience only the best Canterlot High has to offer! And we can’t wait to see you in class!

                            —CHS Welcoming Committee
